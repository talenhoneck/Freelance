Our Services

Most of the time, the birthday party for a little one is more for the parents than the kid. Usually not something a child would remember. As parents, we refuse to believe that is the best we have to offer our little ones on their special day. We want them to celebrate, have fun and center the celebration back where it should be, on our kids. That is why we fell in love with Soft Play area for parties. A soft, safe and clean environment for kids under 5 to explore and enjoy.

.

  • For Infants & Toddlers

    Our play areas are specially designed for children ages 0 to 5 years old.

  • Safe way to play & learn!

    Our soft play equipment promotes gross motor skills development while making play time a safe experience.

  • Indoor or Outdoor

    LilSprouts soft play areas can be setup over any clean, flat, and dry surface, either indoors or outdoors.

  • Oh! So Clean

    We sanitize our equipment before and after play time with residue-free, fragrance-free cleaners.
